ANTWUANDIXON

SkateTap is a Micro Mobility service that makes commercial E - Skateboards and Single seaters available for shared use to individuals on a short term basis.

Many cities already are putting into place different kinds of policies to address the micromobility industry and encouraging the new mobility option. This new form of transportation is seeing the benefits of the first and last mile mobility option that aligns well with many cities goals of reducing congestion and emissions.

The skateboards can be unlocked from one hub and returned to any hub in the system making SkateTap ideal for short one way trips.
